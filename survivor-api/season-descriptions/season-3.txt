Survivor: Africa is the third season of the American CBS competitive reality television series
Survivor. The season was filmed from July 11, 2001 through August 18, 2001 in Kenya's
Shaba National Reserve on the African continent, and aired weekly from October 11, 2001 until the
live finale on January 10, 2002, when Ethan Zohn was named Sole Survivor over Kim Johnson by a
jury vote of 5-2. Hosted by Jeff Probst.
