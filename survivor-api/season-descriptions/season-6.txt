Survivor: The Amazon is the sixth season of the American CBS competitive reality television
series Survivor. The season was filmed from November 11, 2002, through December 15, 2002, in the
Amazon and premiered on February 13, 2003. Hosted by Jeff Probst, it consisted of the usual 39
days of gameplay with 16 competitors.
This was the first Survivor season to initially divide the tribes by gender. The season is noted
for having the first reality show contestant with a disability, the hearing impaired
Christy Smith. The winner, swimsuit model Jenna Morasca, was named Sole Survivor after defeating
Matthew von Ertfelda by a jury vote of 6-1.
