Survivor: Pearl Islands (also broadcast as Survivor: Pearl Islands — Panama) is the seventh season
of the American CBS competitive reality television series Survivor. It was filmed from
June 23, 2003, through July 31, 2003, and premiered on September 18, 2003. The season was filmed
on the Pearl Islands, off the coast of Panama, and had a pirate culture theme. Hosted by
Jeff Probst, it consisted of the usual 39 days of gameplay with 16 competitors.
Office clerk Sandra Diaz-Twine defeated scoutmaster Lillian Morris in a 6–1 jury vote.
